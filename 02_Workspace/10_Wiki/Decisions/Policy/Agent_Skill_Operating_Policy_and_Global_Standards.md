# [[Agent Skill Operating Policy and Global Standards]]

## 📌 한 줄 통찰 (The Karpathy Summary)
> This policy mandates the construction of an autonomous skill system within the workspace to maintain agent operational consistency, ensuring adherence to global branding and standardized data handling.

## 📖 구조화된 지식 (Synthesized Content)
- **추출된 패턴:** Operational stability and predictable quality in autonomous AI systems are achieved by defining, scoping, and rigidly enforcing explicit functional 'Skills' and universal technical standards.
- **세부 내용:**
- **Skill Scope Definition**: Skills must be categorized into 'Global Scope' (project-agnostic brand/design guidelines) and 'Workspace Scope' (project-specific business logic).
- **Design Standards**: All user interfaces must adhere to specific brand guidelines (GEV Blue/Evergreen) and prioritize high-density tables on white backgrounds.
- **Data Integration Protocol**: All future data source connections must standardize on MS 365 SharePoint List, requiring strict adherence to established naming conventions (e.g., Checkout, Return, Tool).
- **Knowledge Persistence**: To ensure compatibility with the Obsidian knowledge base, all operational outputs must be structured in Markdown Table format, guaranteeing knowledge longevity.
