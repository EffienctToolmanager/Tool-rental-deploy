# [[Antigravity Skill Operation Policy and Global Guidelines]]

## 📌 한 줄 통찰 (The Karpathy Summary)
> This policy establishes mandatory standards for autonomous skills, covering scope, UI design, data integration, and output format to ensure agent consistency and professionalism.

## 📖 구조화된 지식 (Synthesized Content)
- **추출된 패턴:** System stability and scalability in agentic workflows require rigid standardization across skill scope, data sources, and output formats.
- **세부 내용:**
- Skills must be organized by scope: Global (company-wide standards) or Workspace (project-specific business logic).
- Brand guidelines mandate the use of GEV Blue (#0047AB) and GE Evergreen themes, favoring high-density white background tables.
- Data connectivity is standardized to use MS 365 SharePoint Lists, requiring strict naming conventions (e.g., Checkout, Return, Tool).
- All operational outputs must be formatted as Markdown Tables to ensure seamless compatibility with the Obsidian knowledge base.
- The goal of this policy is to ensure functional consistency regardless of agent replacement or session restart.
