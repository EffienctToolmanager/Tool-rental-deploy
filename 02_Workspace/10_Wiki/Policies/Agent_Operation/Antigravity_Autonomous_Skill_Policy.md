## 📌 한 줄 통찰 (The Karpathy Summary)
> The policy establishes a structured, two-tiered skill system and mandated technical standards to ensure autonomous agents maintain consistent quality and professionalism across all projects.

## 📖 구조화된 지식 (Synthesized Content)
- **추출된 패턴:** Operational stability for AI agents requires formalized, scope-managed skills, mandatory tooling integration, and strict adherence to cross-platform output formats.
- **세부 내용:**
- The skill system divides functionality into Global Scope (universal design/brand rules) and Workspace Scope (project-specific business logic).
- UI and brand consistency mandates the use of GEV Blue/GE Evergreen themes on white, high-density tables.
- All future database connections must standardize on MS 365 SharePoint Lists, adhering to strict naming conventions.
- Knowledge persistence requires all work results to be outputted in Markdown Table format for optimal Obsidian compatibility.
