# [[Antigravity Skill Operating Policy and Global Guidelines]]

## 📌 한 줄 통찰 (The Karpathy Summary)
> This policy establishes a structured framework for implementing autonomous skills within agent workspaces to ensure operational consistency, adherence to brand identity, and standardized data handling across all projects.

## 📖 구조화된 지식 (Synthesized Content)
- **추출된 패턴:** Operational resilience for AI agents is achieved through mandatory standardization of scope, UI/UX, data sources, and output formats.
- **세부 내용:**
- Skill implementation must utilize a hierarchical scope system: Global Scope (universal brand rules) and Workspace Scope (project-specific business logic).
- Design mandates include utilizing GEV Blue/Evergreen themes and maintaining a clean, high-density white background UI.
- All future database integrations must standardize on MS 365 SharePoint List, strictly following naming conventions (e.g., Checkout, Return, Tool).
- To ensure knowledge persistence and compatibility, all operational outputs must be formatted as Markdown Tables for seamless integration with the Obsidian knowledge base.
